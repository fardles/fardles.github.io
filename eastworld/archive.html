22 August | Oradour, Ordos

Major Updates

  • The party explored the burnt out village of Oradour, encountering the black kami known as Laphroaig and uncovering the truth of what took place there.
    • - While in combat with Laphroaig, Johnni and Fara (through his steed) experienced the final moments of some victims of Oradour - one of whom was slain by a halberd.
    - After Laphroaig's defeat, it was revealed that Kushanite raiders burnt the village down to grow Laphroaig's power through the suffering experienced by the villagers. Laphroaig revealed that the shaman Tosim holds a totem which compels his service, and tasks the party to destroy it.
    - Laphroaig revealed that plans are underway to bring about Karakorum in the city of Ordos, which will take place within a few days. It also revealed that Kushan agents were already in the city.
  • The party returned to Ordos, where the summer festival is underway, seeking to find these agents and prevent the impending calamity.
    • - The party warns both Ambassador Ishar as well as Sergeant Moghe about possible infiltration, stepping up security in the Temple, Market, and Refugee districts.
    - The party enlisteed the help of Safavid in finding out more about Karakorum. Safavid also explained that a once a century astrological event (taking place tomorrow) seems to coincide with this.
    - The party also discovered a cache of tempered Alchemist's Fire (dubbed 'Wildfire' by Johnni and Melissandre within the stores of the Elven Embassy. Ishar explains that they were a 'political gift'.
    - Vinidad spoke to Borthe the Teahouse Owner enquiring about suspicious individuals staying in the Teahouse. She seemed worried about something, and asked the party to come to her again in the evening.

Character Updates

  • Vinidad was on his way to look for Minji when she came rushing to him, desperate for his help.
  • Melissandre and Fara managed to convince the city guard to cancel their holiday and step up security during the festival.
  • Upon meditating on his experiences in the desert, Johnni has developed a way to weaponise the deprivation of water, mastering the ability to use his Ki to Dessicate enemies.
  • Fara has also taken up another sackcloth doll from the ruins of Oradour, in addition to his current doll.

Rumours/Leads

  • The House of Dancing Water is in town, bringing with it a massive carnival. It is currently set up in the Temple District and the adjoining Eastern Market.
  • Through experimentation, the party discovered that "Wildfire" is not easily put out by water, but have found that starving the fire of oxygen does the trick. The party suspects the same substance may have been used in Oradour. The city guard have been notified to prepare sandbags.

15 August | Annapurna, Oradour

Major Updates

  • The party underwent the trial of greed, having their items imbued with various effects.
  • The party underwent the trial of hatred, extinguishing the flames of an elemental burning with hatred. Having completed all three trials, the party recieved a prophecy from Kiki Tiki.
    • - Kiki Tiki prophesied that a time will come when the 'stars will fall from the sky', bringing with it turmoil in Ordos.
    - Kiki Tiki charged the party to uncover the plans of the young Khan leading the Kushanites.
  • The party then set off for the Sherpa village of Oradour.
    • - The party discovers the Shrine dedicated to the Order of Inner Light desecrated, its murals broken, and a statue of the Prabbudh 'beheaded'.
    - The party found the village ruined, with remains burnt to ash. While exploring the village square, the party heard faint whispers, before being beset upon by an unknown force.

Character Updates

  • Vinidad had his Damascus Steel Dagger enchanted into an Igniting Quicksilver Dagger of the Day.
  • Melissandre had her journal enchanted into a Vital Journal of Action and the Doll. Upon waking up the next morning, she discovered a doll by her bed. On writing in her journal "what do you want", she received the reply: "YOU".
  • Johnni had his boomerang enchanted into a Shattering Boomerang of Deceit and Restraint.
  • Fara had a potion of healing enchanted into an Inquisitor's Flask of Sight and the Doll. He too woke up with a doll beside his bed, and has undertaken to write a puppet performance in her honour.

Rumours/Leads

  • Juhani expresses concern to the party regarding Kiki Tiki's pacts.
  • The party discovered that the buildings surrounding the village square are destroyed to their foundations, while the village square was burnt but still standing. Melissandre detected a magical presence within the village square, but is certain the destruction was not caused by this magical source.

8 August | Ordos, Annapurna

Major Updates

  • The party assisted Sergeant Moghe in improving the defence of Ordos, now that her garrisons have departed for Orochi. The party bolstered the existing troops, while putting together a citizen's militia.
    • - Vinidad enlisted the help of Orichenko to have less savoury characters assist in Ordos' defence.
    - Fara applied his military background to train the troops to be prepared for conflict, bolstering them with a rousing speech.
    - Johnni provided the troops with a martial arts demonstration, as well as a rather moving (albeit unexpected) bagpipe performance.
  • The party found out from Safavid that Havir has made headway in researching the shrine at Annapurna, but was late in checking in. The party agreed to visit the shrine - also to cure the curse that inflicted Vinidad.
    • - The party learnt a ritual from Havir involving blue lotus root incense, curing Vinidad of the curse.
    - The party encountered an enigmatic figure called Kiki Tiki, who offered the party 'truth' if they overcame three trials of Hatred, Greed, and Ignorance.
    - The party underwent the trial of Ignorance, all accepting Kiki Tiki's offers.
  • Juhani is setting off for the Sherpa Village, journeying together with the party.

Character Updates

  • Vinidad had a busy few days, setting up a firearms deal with his contact The Merchant and the boss Orichenko. He also then brought Lieutenant Ying into Ordos, under the employ of Orichenko to provide information. Vinidad also accepted Kiki Tiki's bargain to not form personal attachments, in order to protect himself from the pernicious world (reroll any die once per day).
  • Melissandre gave Safavid her thesis - a response to the Sultan's Arcana in the Age of Mechanical Reproduction, which Safavid feels will lead to an official invitation to the Madrasah of Samarkand. Melissandre also accepted Kiki Tiki's bargain, forming a pact with the cosmic powers of beyond for power. (Multiclass warlock with INT as spellcasting attribute, and one additional Invocation).
  • Johnni too accepted a bargain with Kiki Tiki to further his mastery of the elements, giving up his memory of his past motivations for mastering the elements in the first place. Free of such burdensome obligations, he has taken the first step to understanding the void that is the Kai (expended Ki points become Kai points - while at least 1 Kai point is available, Johnni adds his WIS bonus to Attack rolls.)
  • Fara took on an Oath to Kiki Tiki, to serve as guardian not only of the good of the Elves or Dwarves, but for the good of the entire realm, and the natural order. To carry out this oath, Fara was given the Mirror Shield, companion to the Iron Halo (granting its bearer the ability to deflect rays and gaze attacks, as well as an aura of competence granting his CHA bonus to skill checks of allies within 10 feet).

Rumours/Leads

  • The party learnt from The Merchant that the Dwarven Dynasty may not be as committed to the defence of Orochi as initially thought, and that the leader of the Kushan forces deliberately chose Orochi for this reason.
  • Party learnt from Safavid that a popular troupe The House of Dancing Water might arrive in Ordos in a few days, refugees from Orochi.
  • Party learnt from Safavid too that the armaments given to a Western Hero by the gods consisted of a halo, a shield, as well as a flaming sword.

27 June | Ordos, Rudy's Farm

Major Updates

  • Party returned to Ordos with news from Annapurna, informing Safavid of the discovery of the statue of Ishnu within the marble Prabbudh.
    • - The party withheld the details of Safavid's expedition from Ishar, but were nontheless rewarded for assisting Safavid.
    - Juhani was brought to Safavid's residence and remains there as his guest.
    - The party also learnt from Ambassador Ishar that the Dwarven officials have grown increasingly tense lately.
  • Gimmo, Fara, and Vinidad met the Dwarven Captain of the guard, Feng.
    • - From Captain Feng, the party learnt that trouble is brewing in the east near the city of Orochi, with reports of Kushan Raiders amassing.
    - The party was asked to assist the Kennelmaster, Sergeant Moghe, in patrolling the outer perimeter of the city.
  • The Party then travelled with the Geomancer Tarn-Ki towards the foothills of the Dzung Mountain Range, seeking an oasis, while escourting Tenzing and his crew enroute to his archaelogical dig site.
    - Tenzing offered to pay 150gp in total for the escourt, paying half as a deposit. Further compensation will be arranged regarding the artefacts found within the site.
    - The party was introduced to the Halfling Matriarch Rudy, as well as her granddaughter Saoirse. There, they also discovered the Halflings' succulent farm, built around a mountain lake.

Character Updates

  • Melissandre finished reading Arcana in the Age of Mechanical Reproduction, and was compelled to write a thesis supporting the position of arcane magic being more akin to an art. (Gaining for her efforts, a +1 spell save DC for Evocation spells.) She also borrowed books from Safavid's library on the subject of the Great Tengris Khan (for an Elvish, and a Dwarven perspective.)
  • Johnni was introduced to a book Dialogues of Hammadil, a treatise on Dwarven philosophy. Upon reading, the concept of Kai, the absence of Ki, was learnt.
  • Vinidad was led by Minji to meet Orichenko, the boss of an underground organisation operating in Ordos. Orichenko offered two jobs: (1) Securing a location outside the city for the stroage of 'sensitive goods', and (2) Recruitment of a renegade Dwarven Lieutenant who abandoned his post from the city of Orochi.

Rumours/Leads

  • The party learnt from Rudy that Kushanite raiders have been to the settlement weeks ago, buying a large amount of water.

20 June | Annapurna Expedition

Major Updates

  • Party encountered Juhani, a troubled Elf of the Order of Inner Light in the left temple.
    • - Fara tried calming Juhani, even healing Juhani in the midst of combat. The party managed to subdue Juhani, sparing his life.
    - Vinidad and Gimmo discover Juhani's diary, and learn of Juhani's tale: in sparing the lives of 3 Kushan raiders, Juhani believes he caused the death of an entire Sherpa village.
    - Gimmo shared his personal story of letting down his community with Juhani, giving him some hope of redemption. With the party's support, Juhani is on the path to resolving his inner conflict.
    - Melissandre notes that Kushan raiders never venture this far west, and suspects something is amiss.

  • Party explored the right temple, uncovering its ancient mysteries.
    • - Party discovered the Hourglass Chamber, learnt the Inner Light's Litany Against Fear, whispered their fears to the Dwarven Statues, and escaped with the hidden Bronze Mechanism.
    - Using the Bronze Mechanism, the party unlocked the secret door within the Study leading to the Inner Courtyard.
    In the Inner Courtyard, the party discovered a marble Prabuddh statue seated on a six-headed Naga. After some investigation, the party uncovered the Statue of Ishnu within. The party keeps the blue stained head section of the Prabuddh statue to show to Safavid.
    - The party solved the Inner Light Koan, retrieving a Damascus Steel Dagger.

Character Updates

  • Vinidad is in possession of the Damascus Steel Dagger
  • Melissandre and Johnni retrieve some writings from the right building, including the esoteric teachings of this Inner Light sect.

Rumours/Leads

  • The party learnt that the Sherpa Village lies some distance away from Annapurna, and will need some preparation to trek there.
  • Havir will set up an archaelogical dig at the right temple.
