11 July | Rudy's Farm, Taklamakan Desert

Major Updates

  • Party spoke to Rudy, and came to an arrangement to provide water to the city of Ordos, so long as her granddaughter Saoirse is taken care of.
  • Party also learnt about the ants of the farm being stressed and agitated, and investigated the cause with Saoirse. The party discovered the source to be nesting Ankhegs.
    • - The party dispatched both Ankhegs, neutralising the threat to Rudy's Farm. They also discovered a corpse bearing an Amulet with three circles (kept by Gimmo.)
    - Vinidad acquired some acid glands that might be useful in crafting poisons.
    - Gimmo,Johnni, and Vinidad decided to keep an Ankheg egg each, while Rudy kept two for her farm.
    - Gimmo scouted the burrows from which the Ankhegs emerged, finding them to lead south beneath the Taklamankan desert. However, parts of the tunnel were submerged preventing further exploration.
  • Party also met a reclusive Dwarf, Ying, whom the party suspect came from the borderlands near Orochi.
    • - Vinidad asked Melissandre about a Dwarven inscription, which read "Lieutenant".
    - Ying revealed that Kushan forces were amassing near Orochi, and is fearful this might be the beginning of a new Kushan horde. He also asked the party to warn the Dwarven military of the threat.
  • Party then continued south into the Taklamakan Desert, escourting Tenzing's crew. During the first night, an elephant skull was discovered, which Vinidad and Gimmo remembered was linked to fables about the Khan's lost Tomb.

Character Updates

  • Vinidad made an offer to recruit Ying into the organisation. Ying was convinced to think it over at Rudy's Farm.
  • Gimmo discovered that subterranean sources of water might be found around the outskirts of the Tarim Basin.

Rumours/Leads

  • The party learnt from Ying that Kushan raiding squads usually operate in groups of six.
