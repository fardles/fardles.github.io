Shelford Hill

Objects permanent and impermanent

The beggar—
He has heaven and earth
For his summer clothes.

Takarai Kikaku (1661–1707)

These are the objects that inhabit the Hill, together with him and her and them, the objects that they surround themselves with, that surround them, that take on their own lives, controlling and controlled, old and new, being mended and being broken.
